Summary

google-drive-ftp-adater-online is an open source project. It is a web application allowing you to connect your ftp client to your Google Drive account. This website offers that web application as an ONLINE FREE SERVICE available publicly on Internet. The only aim of this website is to provide a NON-PROFIT free service. Your personal data WON'T NEVER BE SHARED TO THIRD PARTIES. The Service is offered from GERMANY data center. The COLLECTED DATA from you includes:
  • EMAIL
  • GOOGLE DRIVE AUTHORIZATION TOKEN
  • LIST OF YOUR GOOGLE DRIVE FILES
  • FTP USER

We use your email for 2 reasons: As a username to login to the website and only if needed, as a way to communicate about any issue concerning the application like changes to this privacy policy or security information. Your email will be never used to send you spam or any commercial data. You will have the right to delete completely your account including all your personal data by sending an email to ftpdrive@andresoviedo.org.

The Google Drive Authorization Token (this is not your google username or password) is a file containing a String allowing the application to connect to your Google Drive account on your behalf. At any time you will have the option (a button) to revoke this authorization yourself in the application.

The list of your google drive files is only a list of file names. It means that if you have a file named "your-picture.jpg" or any other file, the application will save into the application's database the path and the name of that file (e.g. "/your-folder-name/your-picture.jpg"), but it wil never store the picture itself or any binary content. The only purpose of having this list stored in the database is for perfomance reasons to provide the functionality of this service. This information will only be downloaded to application's database the first time you connect your ftp client to the Service. You will have the option to delete completely this data by sending an email to ftpdrive@andresoviedo.org

The FTP user is a user you create yourself in the application to allow FTP clients to connect to your google-drive-ftp-adapter-online account, therefore to your Google Drive account. You will have the option ( a button) to delete this user yourself at any time. It is your reponsability to manage that user. We don't assume any responsability derived from the misuse of the FTP user.

If you register and you don't use the ftp service for a period of 1 year your data will be deleted. An email will be sent to you prior to deletion. You will have the option to respond to this email with a consent to mantain your data for 1 more year.

This Service is free and mantained with no financial support. The Service could be unavailable at any time.

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy please send an email to ftpdrive@andresoviedo.org.

FTPS Protocol

The Service provides 2 methods to connect your FTP client to the Service, either using unencrypted FTP or encrypted FTPS. FTP IS NOT A SECURE PROTOCOL because data is transferred with no encryption, while FTPS IS A SECURE PROTOCOL because data is sent through an encrypted connection.

Help

In order to connect your FTP client to Google Drive please follow these steps:

  • Register to https://ftpdrive.andresoviedo.org/
  • Authorize access to google-drive
  • Create an FTP user
  • Open your FTP client and connect to host "ftpdrive.andresoviedo.org" and port "50000"
  • Enjoy :)

If you have further questions send an email to ftpdrive@andresoviedo.org
